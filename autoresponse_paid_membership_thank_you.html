Photos

We store all our photos on our official Facebook Page. If you have any that you'd like to share, contact our PR Director or post them yourself to our timeline. Feel free to tag them as you see fit!
Facebook Group

RPCV/W's Facebook Group is the informal online forum of the organization where you may post event notices, social requests, housing offers, job opportunities, and anything of general interest or amusement. Only RPCV/W members may post fundraisers and events requiring payment. Please refrain from fundraising for overseas activities or advertising products, services, or events which are political in nature.


Linkedin Group

Join our LinkedIn Group and network with other RPCVs in the city. We work in almost every industry and agency in DC. If you're looking for a job, we recommend you reach out to RPCVs working in places that interest you and browse our jobs page. Most people are happy to sit down with you over coffee and offer their advice. Don't be shy, and be sure to follow up with a thank you note!


NPCA Network Group

If you really want to stay updated on everything Peace Corps, consider joining the NPCA Social Network and joining the group we maintain there.


RPCVW Twitter

Our Twitter account is an active stream of news, commentary, job announcements and updates related to Peace Corps and Washington, DC life in general. If your company or organization has a Twitter account, ask them to follow us too!


 Weekly Newsletter

Our free newsletter goes out every week to over 1200 recipients. Upon registering, we will add your email address to the list. If you are not getting the newsletter, you can sign up here. Any RPCV/W Member can submit items for publication, provided it is not a commercial advertisement or political in nature. Send an email to our Secretary with a short blurb, 50 words or less, a point of contact, and an image (optional).
